###
### How to start VLBI Monitor Client
### 2018/10/18  Satoki Matsushita (ASIAA) instructed by Nimesh Patel (SAO)
###

1. At gltacc
   > cd ~/ehtMonRedis
   > ./ehtMonRedis

2. At glt-thule
   > cd ~/EHT_MC/vlbimonitor-client
   > ./client.py


Note:
   When the telescope at "Stow" position, this "client.py" does
   not work.  Some decoding problem for the "Stow" mode.
